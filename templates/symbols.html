The National Anthem

The anthem of the United Communist Democratic States, "Ode to the People's Will," inspires unity and reminds us of our shared journey and aspirations. Its stirring lyrics reflect our commitment to democratic principles and collective well-being.

"Ode to the People's Will"

(Verse 1)
From humble start, our vision clear and bright,
A people's voice, uniting in the light.
For common good, our future we define,
In UCDS, true freedom's star shall shine.

(Chorus)
Ode to the People's Will, our anthem strong and free,
For unity and liberty, UCDS for thee!
Through struggle, growth, and wisdom's steady hand,
We build our future, strong across the land!

(Verse 2)
No dark ages bind, no tyranny shall stand,
With honest hearts, we build a promised land.
Each voice is heard, each effort finds its place,
Progress our guide, with dignity and grace.

(Chorus)
