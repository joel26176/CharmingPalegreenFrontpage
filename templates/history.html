Our Origins

The United Communist Democratic States (UCDS) was officially created on June 14th, 2024. It was founded by myself, kingstondino, along with a group of close friends who shared a vision for a new micronation.

Growth and Challenges

As UCDS grew, we expanded our community, inviting dedicated individuals like Nooby, @Senat, and @Архангельск to join our ranks and contribute to our development.

However, our early days were not without turmoil. Some initial members, whose ideologies diverged from our communist principles, attempted a revolution and raid. This attempt ultimately failed, and they subsequently left, having betrayed our trust.

The Dark Ages and Rebirth

A challenging period known as the "Dark Ages" ensued when we mistakenly entrusted ownership to a person known as tizian the scammer. This turned out to be a regrettable decision as our group became entangled in scamming activities, which went against everything UCDS stood for.

But the spirit of UCDS prevailed! We successfully broke free from their influence and the dark ages are officially over. We have emerged stronger and more committed to our original ideals.

Our Leadership

Today, UCDS is led by our esteemed president, Desert Eagle, with founder kingstondino continuing to guide our path.
